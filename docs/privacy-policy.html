Privacy Policy for ToDo Creator

Last updated: July 2026

ToDo Creator ("the app") is a personal task management app. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use it.

Short version

The app does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything you create — folders, tasks, settings — stays on your device.

What data the app stores, and where

  • All folders, tasks, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device automatically.
  • The app has no account system, no sign-in, and no backend server. There is nothing for us to collect, because nothing is sent anywhere.

Notifications

If you enable a reminder for a task, the app schedules a local notification directly on your device via the operating system. No reminder data is sent to any server, and the app does not use push notifications.

Backup export & import

The app offers an optional backup feature. When you tap "Export backup," a JSON file containing your data is saved to a location you choose (or shared via your device's own share sheet) — this is entirely under your control. The app itself never uploads this file anywhere. Importing a backup only reads a file you explicitly select.

Third parties

The app does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties, because no data leaves the app.

Children's privacy

Since the app collects no data at all, it does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version adds optional cloud sync), this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will reflect that.
